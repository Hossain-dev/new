Mug Brownie Recipe

For those times that I need something rich, fudgy, and comforting IMMEDIATELY – I turn to this Brownie in a Mug recipe. It is unbelievably easy, and it has me diving into a warm brownie in a matter of minutes. Seriously, LESS THAN 5 MINUTES. Gah!

Ingredients with steps

  1. Start by mixing the dry ingredients in your mug – flour, sugar, cocoa, salt.
  2. Add the chocolate chips – these make our brownie fudgy!
  3. Stir in the wet ingredients – oil, vanilla and milk.
  4. Sprinkle a few extra chocolate chips on top (because, why not?!)
  5. Microwave it for about 90 seconds.
  6. Top it with your favorite garnishes: ice cream, whipped cream, powdered sugar, berries.
  7. Enjoy.
